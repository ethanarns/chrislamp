Carpet Cleaning

We use the low moisture bonnet method. It's been shown to clean as well as steam cleaning and other methods.

Your carpets will look clean and fresh without high dry times and without damaging the carpet and pad with excessive moisture. We also clean area rugs and carpeted stairs.

  • Our method is better than steam cleaning because: If steam cleaning is done improperly carpets can get irreparably damaged and it doesn't clean any better despite being more dangerous. Also, steam cleaners run on gasoline and emit green house gasses.
  • Our method is better than encapsulation because: Although it does encapsulate some of the soil, it also encapsulates carpet fibers. This results in your carpet becoming sticky and getting dirty faster.
  • Our method is better than shampooing because: Similar to encapsulation, shampoo leaves carpets susceptible to getting dirtier carpets faster, because it's not possible to remove all the shampoo. This once again leaves your carpet sticky.
